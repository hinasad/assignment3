Macaroni Recipe

Ingredients

  1. Bacon.
  2. Broccoli.
  3. Green Beans.
  4. Peas.
  5. Carrots.
  6. Onions.
  7. Shredded Chicken.
  8. Cracker Crumbs.

Method

  • Take tomatoes in a blender.
  • Puree till smooth.
  • Bring water in a sauce pan to a boil.
  • Add salt to taste.
  • Add in some oil.
  • Add in pasta.
  • Cook till pasta is cooked. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat butter in a pan.
